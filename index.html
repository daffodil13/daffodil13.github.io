@katasabil

@katasabil is my Instagram account for sharing stories and some words, both motivational and random words. At first I wrote maybe just to follow the trend. But at one point I realized that writing is not only about popularity, that writing is not only about how much my work is read, nor how well my work sells out. For me, writing about how meaningful the writing that I make can leave an impression on the hearts of readers. Even if it's only one sentence, if it can make him feel better why not? Novel "Cahaya di Langit Seoul"

The story of Sabrina Lee is a Muslim woman who grew up in Korea, she built her career to become a script writer. Her status as a Muslim makes her a very special woman. Even though living as a minority is not easy, Sabrina proves that she can be the best version of her life. Kim Hae Kyung, a mysterious male figure that Sabrina met somewhere. They get involved in an incident, and that's what makes them connected to each other. Muhammad Aldo, a simple man from Indonesia who is currently studying in Korea. He was Sabrina's first friend while studying in Indonesia and is now a friend of Sabrina's faith in Korea. You can Find this story on Wattpad
